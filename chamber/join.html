Non-Profit

Non-profit organizations enjoy the same benefits and services as Bronze level members, but at a discounted price. Proof of non-profit status is required when finalizing your application. Non-profits may also opt to register at a Silver or Gold level at a 20% reduced rate. Please call us directly to discuss this.

Organization Fee:
$100.00/year

Additional Members:
$25.00 / ea. / year

Bronze Level

The Bronze Level is a great introductory memebrship option. Includes the following services and benefits:

  • Recognition as a supporting organization at chamber functions.
  • Free 1/8 column advertising space in quarterly newsletter.
  • Listed as a member business on chamber website.
  • 10% discount for booth space at networking events.

Organization Fee:
$195.00 / year

Additional Members:
$35.00 / ea. / year

Back to Membership Selection

Silver Level

The Silver Level demonstrates a higher level of support for the Chamber. The following services and benefits are included:

  • Recognition as a supporting organization at chamber functions.
  • Free 1/4 column advertising space in quarterly newsletter.
  • Discounts on 1/3 and 1/2 column ad spaces.
  • Listed as a member business on chamber website.
  • Placed in rotation for spotlight as Feature Member on chamber home page.
  • 20% discount for booth space at networking events.
  • Preferred table seating at annual chamber gala and dinner event.

Organization Fee:
$275.00 / year

Additional Members:
$40.00 / ea. / year

Back to Membership Selection

Gold Level

The Gold Level demonstrates the ultimate support for the Chamber. The following services and benefits are included:

  • Prominent recognition as a sponsor organization at chamber functions.
  • Free 1/2 page advertising space in quarterly newsletter.
  • Discounts on full page ad spaces.
  • Listed as a member business on chamber website.
  • Placed in rotation for spotlight as Feature Member on chamber home page.
  • 33% discount for booth space at networking events.
  • 20% discount for chamber-sponsored educational event registration.
  • Exclusive table seating at annual chamber gala and dinner event.

Organization Fee:
$400.00 / year

Additional Members:
$50.00 / ea. / year
